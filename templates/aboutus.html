About Us



SAVERS is a community focused on Building the Future of Giving using blockchain technology. Our intention is to support and reward the funding for good cause by creating open, transparent and free access to the revolutionary funding opportunities available within the Ethereum ecosystem.

SAVERS is building a culture of giving that empowers and rewards those who give -- to projects, to society, and to the world. We aim to inspire our community to participate in an ecosystem of collective support, abundance and value-creation.
